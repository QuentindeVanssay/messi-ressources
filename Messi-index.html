Introduction

Lionel Andrés Messi Cuccitini was born on 24 June 1987 in Rosario, Argentina. Following growth problems in his youth, he was spotted by FC Barcelona. Since joining the Catalan club, Lionel Messi has shown the world that legends and records are always destined to fall, becoming the greatest footballer of all time.
